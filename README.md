# Resumate
Resumate is an open-source resume generation application that guides users through a step-by-step process by asking structured questions. Based on the user’s answers, the application automatically generates a complete, professionally formatted résumé. The main goal of Resumate is to simplify the CV creation process — especially for users who have limited experience writing résumés or who prefer a clear, guided workflow.

## Features

Guided, question-based résumé creation
Generate a CV as a downloadable PDF file
Save generated CVs and personal data to the user's internal profile
Multiple ready-made HTML/CSS templates to choose from
Clean and intuitive user interface suitable for beginners and experienced users alike

## Technologies

Frontend: SvelteKit
Backend: Node.js + Express
Templates: HTML / CSS
Infrastructure: AWS

## Resumate – User Guide

Logging In and Creating an Account
Open the following address in your browser: xxx.xxx
You will be directed to the login page.
Enter your email address and password.
#### Click “Log in”.

If you do not yet have an account:
– Select “Create account” and follow the instructions to set up a new user profile.

If you have forgotten your password:
– Click “Forgot password?” and follow the steps to reset your password.

#### Profile Page

After logging in, you will be taken to your profile page, where you can see:
– Your name and email address
– A list of all CVs you have created

On the profile page you can:
– New CV: navigate to the main page to create a new CV
– Edit: open a selected CV for editing
– Download: download a selected CV as a PDF file to your device
– Log out: the button is located in the top right corner

#### Creating and Editing a CV

On the main page, you can fill in your CV details using the form. The fields include:

– Name
– Photo
– Email address
– Work experience and education
– (Additional fields: skills, summary, languages)

On the right side of the page, you’ll see a real-time preview of your CV, allowing you to track how the final document will look as you edit it.

Downloading and Saving Your CV

When you are finished, you can:
– Download your CV as a PDF file to your device
– Save your CV and continue editing it later from the profile page
